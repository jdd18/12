About Us

We first met in the fall of 2007 in AP US. History and began dating a few months later in the winter of 2008. Our first date was extremely impromptu and in hindsight, “high school” – dinner at Houlihan’s (Allison, a seemingly normal 17 year-old, ordered chicken tenders off the kids’ menu!) and a movie (Step Up 2. Sigh.). A word to the wise: if your first date includes seeing a movie together, make sure it‘s not a sequel. Or revolves around dance sequences. But who would have thought we – or Channing Tatum and his agent – would still be going strong all these years later?

Together we traded Hinsdale for Champaign and now, Chicago. And after almost 8 years since that fateful (Tatum-ful) first date, we got engaged in Decemeber 2015. Scott planned an incredibly thoughtful proposal that included both of our families joining us at the Naples Performing Arts Center, where Scott had staged an art gallery celebrating many of our favorite memories together, complete with travel tickets, concert stubs, postcards and other ephemera that he had been saving since 2008. 2008!

We feel so lucky that we have spent so much of lives with each other and are beyond excited to start a new chapter in the book of us, which we hear is a must-read. As the main characters, we may be biased.
